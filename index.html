In the past few years, I've taken on an interest in Broadway and musical theater.

What follows is a list of a few of my favorite Broadway shows.

  • Hamilton
  • Waitress
  • Something Rotten
  • Shuffle Along...

Hamilton

In 2016, my wife and I visited New York City on vacation. Upon arrival, we began entering the lottery to see Hamilton, which was (and is, as of this writing), the hottest show on Broadway - a hip-hop musical about Alexander Hamilton, one of America's Founding Fathers.

On our second day in the city, we won!

We had to cancel our tickets to see The King & I, but it was very much worth it, to get front-row tickets to the most popular show on Broadway for only $20.

Waitress

Music and Lyrics by Sarah Barreiles, and based on the movie by the same name. The movie starred Kerri Russel, but the musical starred Jessie Mueller, who previously won a Tony for playing Carol King in Beautiful.

Something Rotten

Something Rotten is a comedy musical starring Brian D'Arcy James and Christian Borle. It's a hilarious comedy about the (fictional) creation of the first ever musical, that pokes fun at all the musical tropes we all know and love - dance breaks, randomly breaking into song, and the strangely acceptable all-sung shows like Les Miserables.
